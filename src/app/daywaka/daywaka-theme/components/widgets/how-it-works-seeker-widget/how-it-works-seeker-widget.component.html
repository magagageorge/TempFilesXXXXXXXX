How it works

1

Download app

Go to App store and download the app

2

Signup with Woorbi

If you don't have a Woorbi Account,do a quick signup in app

3

Search & Accept Job

Search jobs nearby your location which fits your criteria,capabilities.

4

Go and do the Job

Go to job location and do the job,once done Click "Complete" from the app.

5

Get Paid

You will then receive your money in your preffered payment method
